Eversley v Non-Eversley

The battle of the ages

Eversley versus Non-Eversley the daring battle that has gone on every Friday for a month with the kick-off starting at 13:15.Eversley outclass their local rivals in every department. They have outstanding quality of goals and defending.In an interview with a Eversley player he quoted that this is "probably the most famous fixture in English football".Both Eversley and Non-Eversley have won 2 trophies each, although this could change within the next few weeks.They both draw the all-time series 2-2.
